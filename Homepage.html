About Litlink

Where stories come to life through shared experiences and meaningful conversations among book lovers worldwide.

Our Story

Litlink was born from a simple passion: the love of books and the conversations they inspire. As an avid reader, I noticed how books create invisible threads connecting people across distances and differences. Yet, there wasn't a dedicated space where these connections could flourish naturally.

What started as a personal project to connect with fellow book enthusiasts has grown into Litlink - a platform where every book finds its reader, and every reader finds their community. We believe that stories are meant to be shared, discussed, and cherished together.

Today, Litlink serves thousands of readers worldwide, creating a digital home for book lovers to connect, share insights, and build lasting friendships through their shared love of literature.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant digital community where readers can connect, share insights, and discover new perspectives through the power of shared storytelling.

Our Vision

To become the world's most welcoming platform for book enthusiasts, fostering lifelong reading habits and meaningful literary connections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Litlink

Account & Registration

How do I create a Litlink account? +
Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password that meets our security requirements.
Is Litlink free to use? +
Yes! Litlink is completely free for all users. We believe in making literary communities accessible to everyone.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it? +
Click "Login" then "Forgot Password" on the login modal. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link.

Features & Usage

How do I join a book discussion? +
Navigate to the Discussion Board section, browse through available topics organized by genres or book titles, and click on any discussion to join the conversation.
What are Voice Rooms? +
Voice Rooms are live audio discussions where you can talk with other readers in real-time, similar to a digital book club meeting. You can listen or participate by speaking.
Can I create my own book club? +
Absolutely! You can create a private or public book club, invite members, schedule meetings, and manage discussions all within Litlink.

Privacy & Safety

Is my reading data private? +
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your reading preferences and activity are private by default, and you control what information is visible to other users.
How do I report inappropriate content? +
Use the "Report" button available on all posts, messages, and user profiles. Our moderation team reviews all reports promptly.
