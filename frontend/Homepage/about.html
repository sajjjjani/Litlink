How It All Began

Litlink was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate readers who noticed something missing in the digital reading community. While there were plenty of platforms for reviewing books or tracking reading habits, there was no dedicated space for readers to truly connect with each other.

Our founder, Sarah Chen, an avid book club organizer, realized that the most meaningful literary discussions happened face-to-face. She wanted to recreate that magic online‚Äîa place where you could not only discuss books but also find reading companions who shared your specific tastes.

What started as a small book matching platform has grown into a vibrant community of over 10,000 readers worldwide, all connected by their love of stories.
