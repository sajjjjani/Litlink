How It All Began

Litlink was created as a Final Year Project with a simple vision — to build a dedicated space where readers can connect, share, and discover books together. While there are plenty of platforms for reviewing books or tracking reading habits, there was no single place designed for meaningful reader-to-reader connection.

What started as an academic project quickly grew into something more — a platform where book lovers can find their reading community, join discussions, participate in voice rooms, and discover their next great read through AI-powered matching.

Project Vision

Litlink was built to solve a simple problem: reading is better when shared. The platform brings together everything a reader needs — AI-powered matching to find compatible book lovers, reading circles for group discussions, discussion boards for threaded conversations, voice rooms for real-time talk, and direct messaging for private connections.

Beyond discoverability, Litlink is about building reading communities. Whether you are looking for a deep discussion about literary fiction, a casual book club for fantasy readers, or simply someone who shares your niche genre interests, Litlink helps you find your people.

About the Developer

Litlink is being developed as an individual Final Year Project. The platform is designed to demonstrate how modern web technologies — including real-time communication, AI-powered recommendations, and community-driven features — can come together to create meaningful social reading experiences.

The goal of this project is to explore how technology can strengthen reading communities by making it easier for readers to find each other, share recommendations, and engage in thoughtful literary discussion. Future development will continue to expand the platform's features based on community feedback and evolving reader needs.
