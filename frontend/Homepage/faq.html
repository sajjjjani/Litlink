Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Litlink

How does the AI matching system work?

+

Our AI analyzes your reading preferences, favorite genres, authors, and reading habits to find other users with similar tastes. The algorithm considers multiple factors including:

  • Book ratings and reviews you've shared
  • Genres and authors you follow
  • Your reading activity and discussions
  • Compatibility with other users' reading preferences

Matches are updated regularly as you interact more with the platform.

Is Litlink free to use?

+

Yes! Litlink is completely free for all basic features including:

  • Creating a profile and adding books
  • AI-powered reader matching
  • Text chat with matched readers
  • Joining and participating in discussion boards
  • Basic voice room access

We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core connection experience will always remain free.

Can I create my own book club or discussion group?

+

Absolutely! To create your own group:

  • Go to the Community section
  • Click "Create Group" in the top right corner
  • Choose between a discussion board or voice room group
  • Set your group name, description, and privacy settings
  • Invite members or make it public for anyone to join

Group creators have moderation tools to manage discussions and members.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

+

Your safety is our priority. To report inappropriate behavior:

  • In chat: Click the three dots next to any message and select "Report"
  • In voice rooms: Click the participant's name and select "Report"
  • On profiles: Visit the user's profile and click "Report User"
  • Email us directly at: safety@litlink.com

All reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 24 hours. We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment.

Can I use Litlink on mobile?

+

Yes! Litlink is fully responsive and works on all devices:

  • Mobile Web: Access through any mobile browser
  • iOS App: Available on the App Store (coming soon)
  • Android App: Available on Google Play (coming soon)

All features are available on mobile, including voice rooms and real-time chat.
